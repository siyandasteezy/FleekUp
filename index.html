Our Story

On FLEEK means
absolutely perfect

The Urban Dictionary defines "On FLEEK" as extremely good, attractive, or stylish — and that's exactly the standard we hold ourselves to with every single piece we create.

"We believe luxury shouldn't be imported. South Africa's finest materials, crafted by South African hands."

From our workshop, each ostrich leather bag and animal hide rug is individually made, inspected, and sent out to homes across South Africa and beyond. No shortcuts. No compromises.

How It's Made

From Field to Fashion

Every Fleek Up Home piece begins with a hunt in the South African wilderness and ends in your hands — ethically sourced, lovingly crafted.

01

The Hunt

We source our ostrich and game hides directly from ethical, legal hunts across South Africa's vast open wilderness.

02

The Craft

Each hide is hand-prepared, tanned and dyed — preserving its unique natural grain while unlocking extraordinary colour and texture.

03

The Finish

Master artisans hand-stitch every piece to perfection. No two bags are ever identical — yours is truly one of a kind.
